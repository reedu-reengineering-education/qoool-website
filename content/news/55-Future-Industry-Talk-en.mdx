On 12 March 2026, the [Future Industry Talk](https://wrs.region-stuttgart.de/termine/innovationen-und-zukuenftige-entwicklungen-im-bereich-sensorik/), organised by the Stuttgart Region Economic Development Corporation, took place at the Hahn-Schickard premises. Naturally, quantum sensors were a key topic of discussion. Consequently, the presentation ‘On hybrid sensors and timing technology’ not only provided a brief insight into the functioning and the components required for an NV-based quantum sensor, but also featured a live demonstration with [The Lämp](https://qoool-sensing.org/en/news/14-The-Laemp-en), [Fluoro](https://qoool-sensing.org/en/news/37-Fluoro-final-en) and [Magneto](https://qoool-sensing.org/en/news/40-Meeting-IIS-en). Once again, we received a great deal of positive feedback from industry representatives, who marvelled at our set-ups with enthusiasm.

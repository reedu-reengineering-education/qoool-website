We’re also making progress on the hardware front. At the same time, we’re currently developing the design for the Magneto add-on board for the [senseBox](https://sensebox.de/en/), and the first demo setups will be ready in the coming months – stay tuned 🚀

As you know, we developed our own resonator design because we were dissatisfied with the current state of the art. We also identified a reliable LED light source for our π-MK1 platform, but there was still room for improvement. That is why, over the past few weeks, we have created a completely new light source that has never existed in this form before. We call it a "laser-like LED" because its light spectrum has been optimised and it focuses the light much more effectively onto the diamonds. We still need to decide which diamond we’ll end up choosing. So it remains to be seen 💎

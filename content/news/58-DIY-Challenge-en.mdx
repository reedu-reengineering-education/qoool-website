Our new [Quantum Interactive Section](https://qoool-sensing.org/en/quantum-interactive/lernmaterialien) went live today. In addition to the existing learning and experimentation resources, there are now two new sections: QOOOL Sensing DIY and QOOOL Kit Magneto.

Would you like to know how to build a real NV-based quantum sensor on a breadboard using the [senseBox](https://sensebox.de/) and the QOOOL DIY kit?
At the section [QOOOL Sensing DIY](https://qoool-sensing.org/en/quantum-interactive/diy/quenching), you’ll find a description of a quenching and an ODMR setup, including a circuit diagram, a 3D model for printing yourself, and the code.

Over the next few weeks, the [QOOOL Kit Magneto](https://qoool-sensing.org/en/quantum-interactive/magneto) section will also be updated with new content – so it’s well worth checking back regularly.

The best for last:
You can now join the QOOOL Sensing Community and sign up for our challenge running from **1 June to 31 July 2026**, where you’ll develop your own experiment using the QOOOL DIY Kit, build your setup yourself and submit your documentation. We’ll be awarding prizes for the best entries. 

You can find all the information about the challenge at [https://qoool-sensing.org/en/quantum-interactive/diy/challenge](https://qoool-sensing.org/en/quantum-interactive/diy/challenge)

On Thursday, 22 January 2026, from 1 p.m. to 4 p.m., the [QandAct](https://www.quantumbw.de/de/event/qandact/) event will take place at Arena 2036 (Pfaffenwaldring 19, 70569 Stuttgart). 

We at QOOOL Sensing will be there and invite you to brainstorm, discuss and discover the possibilities of quantum sensor technology for your applications. Come by and see ‘The Lämp’, QOOOL Kit Fluoro and QOOOL Kit Magneto live in action. 

You can register for the event free of charge here: https://eveeno.com/quand-act-2026.

We look forward to seeing you there!

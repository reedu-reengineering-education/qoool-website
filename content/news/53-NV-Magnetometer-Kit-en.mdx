On 3 March 2026, we presented our project and our QOOOL kits to students and staff at a seminar at the 3rd Physics Institute of the University of Stuttgart and at the premises of [ZAQuant](https://www.zaquant.uni-stuttgart.de/), headed by Prof. Dr. Jörg Wrachtrup.

Under the title ‘NV Magnetometer Kit for Digital Education – An Engineering Perspective,’ we showed which components we selected and which technical decisions we made to create the QOOOL Kit Fluoro and Magneto. The subsequent question and answer session and demonstration provided an opportunity for shop talk. The question ‘Whether, when and where you can buy the kit,’ which we have heard several times, shows us that our project and the hardware it produces are also very well received by physicists.

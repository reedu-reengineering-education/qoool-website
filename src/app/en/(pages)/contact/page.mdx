## QOOOL Kit

The QOOOL Kit is designed as a cost-effective extension of the senseBox to make quantum effects tangible and comprehensible.

## Microwave

Microwave excitation is necessary to measure optically detectable magnetic resonance (ODMR) and thus visualize quantum effects. A microwave resonator is being developed for this purpose.

If you have questions about the microwave resonator, the following contacts are available to assist you:
